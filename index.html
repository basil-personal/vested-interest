Frequently asked questions

What is VestedInterest?

We are an income-based student financing option that better aligns the cost of your education with the value you receive. We do that by offering Income Sharing Agreements - in exchange for financing your education, we take a small percentage of future income for a limited time.

The ROI on higher education is becoming more unpredictable for students and the debt burden overwhelming. In particular, the debt burden for professional school students (business, medicine, and law) is growing faster than ever. That's why VestedInterest is focused on providing ISAs for professional students (medicine, law, business, etc.).

How does an ISA with VestedInterest work?

Using dozens of individual factors, we determine your future earning potential and offer a personalized ISA that is competitive with private loans. Our goal is to make sure the amount you pay back over the course of an ISA is on par with a private loan, but structured in a way to minimize student risk and offset payments to later in your career when you're making more money.

Is this better than taking out a loan?

For many students, this is a better option -- although we recommend speaking to someone on our team in order to get a better understanding of your individualized offer.
